What are the evidence-based practices used in physiotherapy clinics in Cork?

Evidence-based practice involves using research findings, clinical experience and client preferences to guide decisions about treatment and management. Common evidence-based practices used in physiotherapy clinics in Cork include manual therapy, exercise therapy, electrotherapy, hydrotherapy, massage therapy and taping techniques.

How can evidence-based practice help me with my condition?

Evidence based practice helps to ensure that patients receive the most effective treatments available for their condition by utilising research findings, clinical experience and client preferences to guide decision making. It also helps to reduce potential harm through reducing unnecessary treatments or interventions which may not be beneficial or could even be dangerous.

Are there any risks associated with evidence-based practice?

There are some risks associated with evidence-based practice as it relies on research that is carried out by experts who have specific knowledge of the area being studied and therefore may not be applicable to all people or conditions. Therefore it is important that practitioners consider this when making decisions about treatment options for individual patients.
